Skin & Cosmetology

Skin & Cosmetology is a specialized branch of medicine that focuses on diagnosing and treating conditions related to skin, hair, and aesthetics. It covers disorders such as acne, eczema, pigmentation, and cosmetic enhancements.

Dermatologists and cosmetologists treat conditions such as skin infections, aging concerns, hair loss, and scars. They also perform procedures like laser treatments, chemical peels, and cosmetic surgeries.

Early diagnosis and proper skincare are crucial for maintaining healthy skin. Regular dermatological consultations and personalized treatments can significantly enhance skin health.

Why Raksha?

  • Laser Hair Removal

  • Hair Loss Treatments & PRP Therapy
  • Minimally Invasive Cosmetic Procedures

Comprehensive Skin & Cosmetology Treatments

Our dermatology and cosmetology department offers personalized treatment plans that include:

  • ✔ Medications for skin conditions and hair care
  • ✔ Minimally invasive procedures for skin rejuvenation
  • ✔ Laser treatments for pigmentation and scars
  • ✔ Cosmetic interventions for skin tightening and lifting
  • ✔ Advanced diagnostic skin analysis for personalized skincare
  • ✔ Personalized skincare regimens for long-term skin health

Skin conditions and cosmetic concerns can impact self-confidence and well-being. At Raksha Hospital, we focus on early diagnosis, advanced treatment options, and holistic skincare to help patients achieve healthy and glowing skin.

Symptoms
  • Acne & pimples
  • Dry or oily skin
  • Hyperpigmentation
  • Itching & rashes
  • Hair thinning or loss
  • Dark circles & wrinkles
Causes
  • Hormonal imbalances
  • Genetics
  • Sun exposure
  • Allergies & infections
  • Poor skincare habits
  • Aging & lifestyle factors
Treatment
  • Topical medications
  • Oral treatments
  • Laser therapy
  • Cosmetic procedures
  • PRP for hair restoration
  • Hydration & skincare regimens
Care
  • Regular dermatology check-ups
  • Using sunscreen daily
  • Following a healthy skincare routine
  • Staying hydrated
  • Avoiding harsh chemicals
  • Maintaining a balanced diet
