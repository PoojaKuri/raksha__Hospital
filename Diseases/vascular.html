Vascular & Endovascular Surgery

Vascular and Endovascular Surgery is a specialized branch of medicine that focuses on diagnosing and treating conditions related to the arteries, veins, and lymphatic system. It includes both traditional surgical procedures and minimally invasive endovascular techniques.

Vascular surgeons treat conditions such as varicose veins, deep vein thrombosis (DVT), peripheral artery disease (PAD), aortic aneurysms, and carotid artery disease. They provide specialized treatments including angioplasty, stent placement, and vascular bypass surgery.

Early detection, advanced surgical techniques, and lifestyle modifications are crucial for improving vascular health and preventing life-threatening complications.

Comprehensive Vascular & Endovascular Treatments

Our vascular surgery department offers personalized treatment plans that include:

  • ✔ Medications for blood clot prevention & circulation improvement
  • ✔ Angioplasty & stent placement for blocked arteries
  • ✔ Minimally invasive catheter-based procedures for vascular health
  • ✔ Bypass surgery for severe artery blockages
  • ✔ Compression therapy for varicose veins
  • ✔ Advanced imaging & diagnostics for vascular assessment

Vascular diseases can significantly impact mobility and overall health. At Raksha Hospital, we focus on early diagnosis, cutting-edge treatments, and preventive care to help patients maintain optimal vascular health and reduce the risk of complications.

Symptoms
  • Leg pain & cramping
  • Swelling in the lower limbs
  • Varicose veins & skin discoloration
  • Cold or numb extremities
  • Non-healing wounds on legs or feet
  • Sudden weakness or dizziness
Causes
  • High cholesterol & hypertension
  • Smoking & obesity
  • Diabetes & poor circulation
  • Genetic predisposition
  • Blood clots & arterial blockages
  • Lack of physical activity
Treatment
  • Medications for vascular health
  • Minimally invasive endovascular surgery
  • Angioplasty & stent placement
  • Varicose vein laser treatments
  • Surgical bypass for severe cases
  • Compression therapy & lifestyle changes
Care
  • Regular vascular check-ups
  • Maintaining a healthy weight
  • Staying active & exercising
  • Managing blood pressure & cholesterol
  • Avoiding smoking & excessive alcohol
  • Following a heart-healthy diet
